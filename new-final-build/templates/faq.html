FAQs

The model provides an accuracy of 92% in determination of pneumonia as well as an accuracy of 78% in categorizing it into Bacterial or Viral Pneumonia.

The analysis was created as a primary step for detecting the possibility of pneumonia remotely and should not be used as a prescription. If Pneumonia is detected, please visit a specialist regarding guidance about further steps. We are not liable for an complications which may occur due to self-diagnosis based on this analysis.

A machine learning model (software) was trained by feeding numerous x-rays so as to detect the presence and type of Pneumonia. It then accepts your uploaded X-ray and gives the detected output as Pneumonia Negative (Normal) or Pneumonia Positive (Bacterial or Viral).
